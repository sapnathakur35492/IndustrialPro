Electric Resistance Furnace

Engineered by SKR Engineering for reliability and low maintenance, the electric furnace range is ideally suited for light to medium capacity foundries, die casting units, and gravity casting operations.

Bale Out Furnace

Hydraulic Tilting Electric Furnace · Bale Out / Tilting Electric variant

A bale out furnace is a specialised holding and dispensing furnace designed to maintain molten aluminium at precise casting temperatures and deliver consistent metal to the casting station. The hydraulic tilting electric furnace is the best match for bale-out electric applications.

Particularly favoured in high-volume die casting and gravity casting operations where uninterrupted metal supply is essential.

Skalner Furnace

Gas Fire Reverberatory · Closest match for Skalner / large capacity shaft melting

The Skalner / reverberatory furnace is an advanced aluminium melting system engineered for superior energy efficiency and minimal metal loss — critical parameters that impact foundry profitability.

Preferred for operations processing aluminium scrap, returns, and secondary alloys where metal recovery rates are a critical commercial consideration.

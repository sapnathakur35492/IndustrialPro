About Filman Filters

Filman Filtrations Pvt. Ltd. manufactures industrial filtration solutions — air filters, oil/hydraulic filters, air-oil separators, gas-oil separators, dust collector filters, and vacuum filtration assemblies for foundry, automobile, and process industries.

01

Industrial Air Filters

Industrial air filters protect process equipment, compressed air systems, and personnel environments from metal dust, sand fines, and atmospheric contaminants.

Range covers panel, cartridge, and bag filters for process air intake, HVAC, compressed air pre-filtration, and enclosure ventilation.

02

Oil / Hydraulic Filters

Contaminated hydraulic fluid is a leading cause of system failure. Filman hydraulic / oil filters protect pumps, valves, and actuators in die casting machines, presses, CNC equipment, and power units.

03

Air-Oil Separators

Air-oil separators remove entrained oil and aerosols from compressed air and process streams — critical for pneumatic lines and compressor systems.

04

Gas-Oil Separators

Gas-oil separators for ammonia chillers and refrigeration systems — engineered for reliable oil separation with minimal pressure drop.

05

Dust Collector Filters

Cartridge dust collector filters for shot blasting, sand casting, and grinding stations — high-grade synthetic polyester media with earthing provision for non-flammability and long service life.

06

Vacuum Filters

Exhaust oil mist separators and vacuum pump filtration compatible with Busch, Leybold, Edwards, Elmo Rietschle and more — glass fibre media with oil carryover as low as 3–4 ppm.

About Filman Filters

Filman Filters Pvt. Ltd., based in Pune, is a well-established Indian manufacturer of industrial filtration solutions serving a diverse range of process industries. With a product portfolio spanning air filtration, hydraulic filtration, fluid separation, and dust collection, Filman Filters brings engineering-grade filtration solutions that meet the rigorous demands of foundry, automobile, food processing, petrochemical, and general manufacturing environments.

01

Industrial Air Filters

Industrial air filters are essential wherever clean, uncontaminated air is required for process equipment, compressed air systems, or personnel environments. In foundry and manufacturing settings, airborne particulates — metal dust, sand fines, combustion by-products, and atmospheric contaminants — pose a significant threat to equipment reliability, product quality, and operator health.

The range covers panel filters, cartridge filters, and bag filters suitable for process air intake protection, HVAC systems, compressed air pre-filtration, and equipment enclosure ventilation. Filter media options include synthetic fibre, glass fibre, and cellulose.

02

Hydraulic Filters

Hydraulic systems are the lifeline of modern industrial machinery — and contaminated hydraulic fluid is the single largest cause of hydraulic system failure globally. Particles as small as 5–10 microns can cause accelerated wear of pumps, valves, and actuators, leading to costly downtime and component replacement.

The range includes suction strainers, return line filters, pressure line filters, and offline (kidney loop) filtration units. Filman hydraulic filters are widely used in die casting machines, press brakes, injection moulding machines, CNC equipment, and industrial hydraulic power units.

03

Separators

Separators are used to remove entrained liquids, solids, or gases from process streams — a critical requirement in compressed air systems, pneumatic lines, fuel systems, and process fluid circuits. Filman's separator range covers oil-water separators, condensate separators, and centrifugal separators — each designed to remove contaminants efficiently without significant pressure drop.

In compressed air applications, separators remove bulk water and oil aerosols before downstream filtration. In industrial fluid systems, oil-water separators treat contaminated condensate before discharge — ensuring environmental compliance and preventing costly effluent penalties.

04

Dust Collectors

Industrial dust collection is a health, safety, and environmental necessity in foundry, machining, and fabrication environments. Airborne metal dust, sand particles, and grinding debris must be captured at the source and collected efficiently to protect operator health, prevent equipment contamination, and comply with workplace air quality standards.

Filman's dust collector range covers bag house filters, cartridge dust collectors, and cyclone pre-separators — designed for high-volume dust-laden air streams. In foundry environments, dust collectors are essential at shakeout stations, sand mixing areas, grinding stations, and furnace charging points. Units are available as standalone floor-mounted systems or modular installations.

About Foseco & Vesuvius

A Legacy Born from Innovation

The story of Foseco begins in 1932 in Birmingham, UK. Today Foseco operates as the foundry division of Vesuvius PLC — serving foundries across more than 30 countries. As an authorised Foseco channel partner, Multi Sales Corporation brings this world-class portfolio to foundries across Maharashtra.

01

Crucibles & Retorts

Foseco delivers crucible solutions engineered for demanding foundry applications — exceptional thermal shock resistance, extended service life, and optimal metal quality.

Two principal families: Salamander® (VAluStar, ISO-Alustar Z2, ISO-Alustar e²) and Noltina® (Syncarb® Z2, Syncarb® FL).

Key Benefits
  • Exceptional thermal shock resistance
  • Reduced metal loss and dross generation
  • Extended crucible life
  • ENERTEK energy-efficient options
02

Fluxes for Aluminium Alloys

Foseco's COVERAL flux range covers cleaning and drossing applications in aluminium foundries. They reduce oxide formation, help remove non-metallics, and reduce aluminium content of dross.

Also available: grain refiners (NUCLEANT, TIBORAL, ELDUCTAL) and SIMODAL modifiers for aluminium-silicon alloy treatment.

03

DYCOTE Die Coatings

Die coatings control filling and solidification, facilitate casting release, and prolong die life in gravity and low-pressure die casting.

Application: Spray (preferred), brush, or immersion. Includes DYCOTE main range and DYCOTE SAFEGUARD.

04

Degassing Machines (FDU)

Foseco's FDU (Foundry Degassing Unit) uses rotary degassing with a patented rotor to remove dissolved hydrogen — the primary cause of porosity in aluminium castings.

Also available: Roto-Schwenk degassing unit for flexible melt treatment.

05

Metal Treatment Station (MTS)

The MTS 1500 builds on FDU rotary degassing with flux dispensing into a controlled vortex for highly efficient melt treatment.

Improved melt cleanliness
Reduced consumption
Lower treatment costs
SMARTT Degassing
06

RPT & Density Index Machines

Reduced Pressure Testing (RPT) and Density Index (DI) quantify hydrogen content and porosity tendency before casting. Together with FDU degassing and COVERAL fluxes, they form a complete melt quality loop — treat, test, verify, cast.

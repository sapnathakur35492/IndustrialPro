THERMOL — Combustion Catalyst

THERMOL is a multifunctional combustion catalyst developed in collaboration with the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Mumbai. It is formulated for use with liquid fuels including Furnace Oil, Low Sulphur Heavy Stock (LSHS), and Light Diesel Oil (LDO) — the fuels most commonly used in oil fired industrial furnaces, boilers, and thermal equipment.

THERMOL's ingredients include surfactants, dispersants, solvents, and proprietary combustion catalysts — all organic and metal-free, making it safe for use across a wide range of industrial fuel systems without risk of equipment contamination or ash deposition.

Just 1 litre of THERMOL treats 2,000 litres of fuel oil — making it highly cost-effective.

How THERMOL Works

THERMOL functions at the molecular level within the fuel — improving the dispersion of fuel droplets during atomisation, reducing incomplete combustion, and catalysing the oxidation reaction within the combustion zone. The result is a more complete, efficient burn that extracts greater heat energy from every litre of fuel consumed while generating fewer harmful by-products.

Benefits

  • Improves combustion — more complete burn and higher thermal output
  • Optimises air requirement — leaner combustion without loss of flame stability
  • Prevents sludge formation — cleaner tanks and fuel lines
  • Changes oxidation states of vanadium, reducing SOx emissions
  • Reduces scale formation at heat exchanging surfaces
  • Reduces high and low temperature corrosion of furnace internals
  • Reduces overall fuel oil consumption

Why It Matters for Foundries

Oil fired aluminium melting furnaces are among the highest fuel-consuming equipment in a foundry. Even a 3–5% improvement in fuel efficiency translates into significant cost savings at scale — plus longer refractory life and lower maintenance downtime.

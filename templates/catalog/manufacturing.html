01

Aluminium Handwheels

Valve & Equipment Operation

Aluminium handwheels are a fundamental manual control component in industrial process plants, valve assemblies, and machine tools — used wherever an operator needs to apply rotational force to open, close, or adjust a valve, gate, press, or mechanical stop.

Multi Sales Corporation manufactures aluminium handwheels using gravity die casting — a process ideally suited to the spoke-and-rim geometry of handwheels, delivering clean surface finish, accurate dimensional repeatability, and the light weight that makes frequent manual operation comfortable and fatigue-free. Compared to cast iron alternatives, aluminium handwheels offer 65–70% weight reduction while maintaining adequate strength for standard valve and equipment operation.

02

Aluminium Pulleys

Aluminium pulleys are a critical mechanical transmission component used wherever rotational power must be transferred between shafts via belt drives — from industrial machinery and HVAC systems to agricultural equipment and automotive engines.

Multi Sales Corporation manufactures aluminium pulleys in a range of configurations — V-belt pulleys, flat belt pulleys, timing pulleys, and multi-groove variants — using gravity die casting with post-cast machining to achieve the tight dimensional tolerances and surface finishes required for reliable, vibration-free operation.

03

Aluminium Couplings

Shaft couplings are the mechanical interface between two rotating components — transferring torque from a driving shaft to a driven shaft while accommodating misalignment, absorbing shock loads, and protecting connected equipment from dynamic overload.

Multi Sales Corporation manufactures aluminium couplings across several standard and custom configurations — rigid couplings, jaw (spider) couplings, flange couplings, and disc couplings — using gravity die casting with precision CNC machining of bore, keyway, and mating faces.

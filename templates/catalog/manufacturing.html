Our Capability

Manufacturing Excellence

At Multi Sales Corporation, manufacturing is where decades of metallurgical expertise meet modern casting practice. We produce high-quality aluminium castings across three primary product families: Aluminium Wheels / Handwheels, Pulleys, and Couplings — serving customers across India and exporting to Sri Lanka, Argentina, UAE, Bahrain, Oman, United States, and Germany.

01 · Aluminum Wheels

Aluminium Handwheels

Valve & equipment operation handwheels — gravity die cast for clean finish, dimensional repeatability, and 65–70% weight reduction versus cast iron.

02

Aluminium Pulleys

Belt-drive power transmission components — V-belt, flat belt, timing, and multi-groove variants — gravity die cast with post-cast machining for tight tolerances.

03 · Aluminum Couplings

Aluminium Couplings

Shaft couplings for torque transfer with misalignment capability — rigid, jaw, flange, and disc configurations with precision CNC machining of bore, keyway, and mating faces.

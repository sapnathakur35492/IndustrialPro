About Our GDC Machines

Gravity Die Casting (GDC) delivers superior dimensional accuracy and surface finish for aluminium castings. Multi Sales Corporation develops custom GDC machines in collaboration with Alucast — engineered to your casting geometry, volume, and plant layout.

01

Horizontal GDC Machine

Designed for castings with a horizontal parting line — ideal for flat, disc, or plate-type components. Rigid fabricated steel frame, precision guide pillars, and hydraulic die actuation.

02

Tilting GDC Machine

Preferred for low-turbulence filling of structural and pressure-tight components. Servo/hydraulic tilt drive with programmable tilt profiles for process repeatability.

  • Controlled, low-turbulence filling
  • Reduced porosity and oxide entrapment
  • Superior mechanical properties

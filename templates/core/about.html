Our History

The Foundation of Expertise

Multi Sales Corporation was founded in 1999 by Mr. Anil Jadhav, a mechanical engineer who graduated from the prestigious Kolhapur Institute of Technology and further strengthened his academic credentials with a Diploma in Computer Applications from CSiber. However, it was not textbooks alone that shaped his vision — it was decades of hands-on immersion in the industrial world. With over 30 years of experience spanning metallurgy, foundry operations, and precision machining, Mr. Jadhav developed an intimate understanding of what manufacturing companies truly need: reliable supply, consistent quality, and a partner who speaks the language of the shop floor.

Growth

Building a Regional Legacy

What began as a focused trading and distribution venture quickly evolved into a trusted name across Maharashtra's industrial belt. Strategically headquartered in Kolhapur — a thriving industrial hub at the crossroads of major manufacturing centres including Pune, Mumbai, Aurangabad, Nasik, Hubli, Bengaluru, and Goa — Multi Sales Corporation was uniquely positioned to serve a dense and diverse industrial ecosystem.

Over the years, the company grew its footprint across more than five districts, forging long-standing relationships built on technical credibility, assured quality, and dependable after-sales service. From aluminium foundry materials and machinery to industrial filtration systems, the company's portfolio expanded in lockstep with its customers' evolving needs.

25+ Years

A Quarter-Century of Industrial Partnership

Today, with more than 25 years in operation, Multi Sales Corporation stands as one of the region's leading suppliers of world-class industrial equipment, materials, and components. The company serves a cross-section of industries including foundries, food & beverage, petrochemicals, automobile manufacturing, and technology. It operates company-owned warehouses to ensure smooth logistics and supply continuity, and has extended its reach beyond Indian borders — exporting aluminium and cast iron castings to Sri Lanka, Argentina, and Gulf countries.

This enduring legacy is a testament to Mr. Anil Jadhav's founding belief: that deep domain knowledge, paired with honest business practice, is the most durable competitive advantage.

The Next Chapter

Carrying the Legacy Forward

The second generation of leadership at Multi Sales Corporation is represented by Mr. Mohit Jadhav, who brings with him a powerful combination of engineering acumen and strategic business thinking. A graduate of Electronics Engineering and holding a Master of Business Administration in Marketing — both from Pune University — Mr. Mohit Jadhav has spent over a decade in management consulting, advising organisations on growth strategy, market positioning, and operational efficiency.

He is also the founder of DataHorizzon Research, a renowned and credible market research firm that serves clients globally — a venture that reflects his deep analytical capabilities and entrepreneurial drive.

A New Vision for an Established Institution

Under Mr. Mohit Jadhav's growing influence, Multi Sales Corporation is evolving from a well-established regional supplier into a future-ready industrial enterprise. His exposure to global markets, data-driven decision-making, and consulting methodology is being channelled into modernising the company's operations, expanding its product lines, and building stronger international partnerships.

The convergence of Mr. Anil Jadhav's on-the-ground industrial mastery and Mr. Mohit Jadhav's strategic and analytical perspective creates a rare and powerful leadership synergy — one that positions Multi Sales Corporation for its next phase of growth with both confidence and clarity.

Industries We Serve

Over 25 years, Multi Sales Corporation has built deep domain knowledge across a diverse range of industries.

Foundries & Metallurgy

Our roots lie here. We supply a comprehensive range of aluminium foundry materials, foundry machinery, and process consumables to foundries of all scales. With Mr. Anil Jadhav's 30+ years of firsthand metallurgical expertise, we are more than a supplier — we are a technical partner on the foundry floor.

Automobile & Auto Components

The automobile sector demands precision, consistency, and zero-compromise quality. We supply critical industrial materials and components to auto ancillary manufacturers and Tier 1 & Tier 2 suppliers operating across the Pune-Kolhapur-Aurangabad manufacturing corridor.

Food & Beverage

Hygiene, compliance, and reliability are non-negotiable in food processing. We supply industrial filters and process equipment that meet the stringent standards required by food and beverage manufacturers in the region.

Petrochemical & Process Industries

We serve petrochemical plants and process industry operators with industrial filtration systems and materials engineered for demanding, high-temperature, and chemically aggressive environments.

Technology & Engineering

From precision machined components to specialised industrial materials, we support engineering and technology companies requiring consistent, high-specification supply with dependable logistics backing.

Export — Castings & Components

We export high-quality aluminium and cast iron castings to industrial buyers across multiple continents — South Asia, Middle East, Americas, and Europe — offering competitive pricing, reliable quality, and end-to-end documentation support.

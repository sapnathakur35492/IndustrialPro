Multi Sales Corporation's manufacturing facility in Kolhapur is home to a fully integrated foundry and precision machining unit. Spanning a well-equipped production floor, the facility is designed to support high-volume aluminium casting production while maintaining ISO-certified quality standards at every stage.

From raw aluminium intake and melting through Gravity Die Casting, finishing, and final inspection — our facility offers an end-to-end production capability tailored for both domestic supply and international export requirements.

Infrastructure Highlights

Our facility is equipped with industry-leading machinery, precision tooling, and controlled process environments to deliver consistent, high-quality castings.

Aluminium Melting Unit

Electric and gas-fired aluminium melting furnaces — sourced from SKR Engineering — capable of precise temperature control for consistent melt quality.

  • Precise temperature management
  • High melt-rate capacity
  • Reduced dross and wastage

GDC Casting Setup

Horizontal and tilting Gravity Die Casting machines developed in-house in collaboration with Alucast — optimised for aluminium casting of handwheels, pulleys, and couplings.

  • Horizontal & tilting variants
  • High dimensional accuracy
  • Consistent cycle times

Degassing & Melt Treatment

Foseco FDU Degassing equipment integrated into the melt treatment process — ensuring low hydrogen content and superior mechanical properties in final castings.

  • FDU Rotary Degassing
  • Reduced porosity
  • RPT / Density Index testing

Precision Machining

Dedicated machining section for post-casting finishing operations — turning, drilling, and milling to final tolerances for export and domestic customers.

  • CNC & conventional machines
  • Tight dimensional tolerances
  • Export-ready finishing

Quality Control Lab

In-house quality inspection at every production stage — dimensional checks, visual inspection, and melt quality analysis using RPT (Reduced Pressure Test) equipment.

  • ISO-compliant QC process
  • Stage-by-stage inspection
  • Documentation for export

Storage & Dispatch

Organised raw material and finished goods storage with efficient dispatch logistics — supporting both domestic distribution and international export packing requirements.

  • Segregated raw & finished stores
  • Export-grade packaging
  • Timely delivery track record

Our Production Process

A streamlined, quality-controlled workflow from raw material to finished casting.

01
Raw Material Intake

Aluminium ingots & alloys received, inspected, and segregated by grade

02
Melting & Treatment

Melted in SKR furnaces; degassed & flux-treated using Foseco products

03
GDC Casting

Poured into GDC dies; shaped into handwheels, pulleys & couplings

04
Machining

CNC & conventional finishing to dimensional tolerances

05
QC & Dispatch

Final inspection, documentation, and export-ready packaging

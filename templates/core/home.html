Multi Sales Corporation

Founded in 1999 by Mr. Anil Jadhav — a mechanical engineer with over 30 years of hands-on expertise in metallurgy, foundry operations, and precision machining.

Strategically headquartered in Kolhapur — a thriving industrial hub at the crossroads of major manufacturing centres including Pune, Mumbai, Aurangabad, Nasik, Hubli, Bengaluru, and Goa — Multi Sales Corporation has grown into one of the region's leading suppliers of world-class industrial equipment, materials, and components.

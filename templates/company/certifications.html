Authorized Dealer — SKR Engineering

Multi Sales Corporation is an authorized dealer for SKR Engineering — a Bengaluru-based industrial furnace manufacturer with over 25 years of engineering excellence. Established in 1998 and headquartered at the KIADB Hoskote Industrial Area in Bangalore, SKR Engineering specialises in designing and producing industrial furnaces for a wide range of applications.

Their product range includes Aluminium Melting cum Holding Furnaces (electrical, oil-fired, diesel, and gas variants), Hydraulic Tilting Furnaces, Reverberatory Furnaces, Heat Treatment Furnaces, Auto Loaders, Crucible Lifters, and the Smart Saver Metal Reclamation Machine. With over 500 satisfied customers across India, SKR Engineering has built a strong reputation for design quality, cost efficiency, and prompt delivery.

As their authorized regional dealer, Multi Sales Corporation brings SKR Engineering's full furnace portfolio directly to foundries and aluminium manufacturers across Maharashtra — supported by our own on-ground technical expertise and after-sales service network.
